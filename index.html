A member-driven cooperative society (Souharda Sahakari), registered to provide financial services to its members in Mysuru district. Official details on specific services are not yet published online, as the society's website indicates it is "opening shortly".

Rashtriya Samruddhi Souharda Sahakari Sangha Niyamita, Mysuru is a cooperative institution established for the economic empowerment of small wage workers, the working class, and the general public. The main objective of our organization is to provide easy and reliable loan facilities to those in need, while also educating members about financial discipline and the importance of savings. Encouraging entrepreneurship among youth and supporting them in starting their own businesses through financial assistance, guidance, and motivation is one of our key missions. Our goal is to help members lead self-reliant lives and contribute to the overall economic development of society. Trust, transparency, and a spirit of service are the core values of our organization.
